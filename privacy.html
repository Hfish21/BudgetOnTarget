BudgetOnTarget

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

BudgetOnTarget is a self-hosted application. Your financial data never leaves your machine. There is no cloud, no telemetry, no analytics, and no third-party data sharing.

Data You Provide

When you use BudgetOnTarget, you import bank CSV files containing transaction data. This data is stored locally in a SQLite database on your machine. It is never transmitted to any external server, API, or third party.

What We Don't Collect

  • No personal information
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No cookies or tracking
  • No account creation or authentication
  • No cloud storage or sync

This Landing Page

This website (budgetontarget.com) is a static page hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, referring page) as described in GitHub's Privacy Statement. We do not add any additional tracking, analytics, or cookies to this page.
