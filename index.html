BudgetOnTarget

A self-hosted budget dashboard that turns bank CSV exports into actionable spending insights. Your data stays on your machine.

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

🎯

Target-Based Budgeting

Set spending caps and income floors per category with tolerance bands. Track performance with green/yellow/red status at a glance.

📈

Trends & History

Multi-month views with grouped bar charts, net cash flow tracking, and per-lane scorecards showing averages and deltas.

🔒

100% Private

Runs locally with SQLite. No cloud, no accounts, no subscriptions. Your financial data never leaves your machine.

📄

CSV Import

Import bank CSV exports with automatic deduplication and sign normalization. Ships with USAA parser, easy to add others.

⚙️

Auto-Categorization

Rule-based engine matches transactions to categories using substring or regex patterns. 30+ default rules included.

👁

Privacy Mode

One-click blur on all financial figures across every page. Share your screen or take screenshots without exposing numbers.

Up and running in 3 commands

Docker Compose handles everything — backend, frontend, database setup, and seeding.

$ git clone https://github.com/Hfish21/BudgetOnTarget.git
$ cd BudgetOnTarget
$ docker compose up --build
